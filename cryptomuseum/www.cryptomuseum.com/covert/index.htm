Covert equipment
This section is about equipment used for covert operations, ranging from cold-war spy cameras to state-of-the-art body wearable surveillance transceivers. Covert operations are often thought to be clandestine or illegal activities, but may well be regular surveillance tasks, observations, bugging, protection (of people), etc. Click any of the thumbnails below to enter a category.
